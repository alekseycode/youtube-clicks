Privacy Policy for YouTube Click Tracker

YouTube Click Tracker does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

All statistics, including daily counts, work-hour counts, and streak information, are stored locally on the user's device using Chrome's built-in storage.

The extension monitors navigation events to YouTube solely to count intentional visits. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

This extension operates entirely offline and does not use remote code.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer through the extension's GitHub repository.
